Premises Hire

The church has three spaces available for hire.

Main space

This can seat about 80 people and has a small platform and carpeted floor.

Bar/Kitchen area

This is a rectangular room suitable for meetings of up to about 15 people. We have a domestic cooker, microwave, three kettles, small fridge and a small freezer.

Meeting room

This room is accessed through the main room and could be used for interviews, small group discussions, counselling sessions etc. It has windows on to the main hall with curtains.

Please use the contact form or call the church to request more information.

• The Bar/Kitchen area £15 per hour

• Meeting room £10 per hour

• Main room £35 per hour
